## 💡 Fiverr Gig 优化技巧

### 1. Gig 图片怎么做
- 用你的 Streamlit Cloud 链接截图
- 用 OBS 录 15-30 秒 demo GIF
- 第一个图最重要（决定点不点进来）

### 2. 前 3 单怎么拿
- 定价降到 **$10-15**（亏本换好评）
- 在 description 最后写：*"First 3 clients get 50% off — message me for a custom offer"*
- 拿到 3 个 5 星好评后恢复原价

### 3. 关键词标签（Fiverr 最多 5 个）
- Gig 1: `ai chatbot`, `chatbot development`, `openai`, `customer support`
- Gig 2: `ai pdf`, `document analysis`, `pdf summarizer`, `ai assistant`
- Gig 3: `ai content`, `content generator`, `seo writing`, `copywriting`
- Gig 4: `data analysis`, `ai dashboard`, `data visualization`, `business intelligence`
- Gig 5: `ai comparison`, `multi model`, `ai compiler`, `model evaluation`

### 4. 响应时间
- Fiverr 看重响应速度，手机装 Fiverr App，1 小时内回复
- 回复模板：*"Thanks for reaching out! Let me understand your needs better. [问 1-2 个问题]"*

## 📨 Upwork Proposal 模板

> Upwork 不是挂 Gig 等人来买，是**主动投 proposal**。以下是模板：

### 模板 A：快速投（300 字以内）

```
Hi [Client Name],

I saw your job post about [briefly restate their need]. I've actually built something very similar — here's a live demo: [your Streamlit Cloud link].

Here's how I'd approach this:

1. [Step 1 — 技术方案，1 句话]
2. [Step 2 — 功能，1 句话]
3. [Step 3 — 交付，1 句话]

I can deliver a working prototype in [X] days.

Questions:
- [问一个具体问题，证明你读了需求]
- [再问一个]

Looking forward to hearing from you!

— JunYit
```

### 模板 B：详细投（有类似作品时）

```
Hi [Client Name],

I read your requirements carefully. You need [summary of their need].

I recently built a similar AI tool — [describe your project briefly]. You can test it here: [link].

My approach for your project:

🔹 Tech: [Streamlit / Python / OpenAI API]
🔹 Key Features: [list 3-4 specific features]
🔹 Timeline: [X] days to prototype, [X] days to polish
🔹 Price: $[XX] fixed price

I have 3 AI applications live in production. I focus on clean code and fast delivery — you'll get a working app, not just a prototype.

Happy to jump on a quick call to discuss. What time works for you?

Best,
JunYit
```

### 什么时候投哪种？
- 预算 < $50 → 模板 A（快速投，拼速度）
- 预算 $50-200 → 模板 B（展示作品）
- 预算 > $200 → 模板 B + 附上 1 页 PDF 方案
